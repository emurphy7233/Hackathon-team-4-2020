About

Volunteer with Animals

With staying in the guidelines of social distancing, it is now possible to actively get involved with your local animal welfare organisation to help with a variety of tasks such as:

  • Feeding schedules
  • Enclosure cleaning
  • Care of injured animals
  • Administration assistance
  • And much more!

Become an Animal Ambassador

Register as an ambassador with us, and become an integral part of our networking team, helping to spread awareness in your local community and on wider social media. As part of this programme, you’ll receive a certificate status as a life-long member.

Adopt an Animal

Adopt an animal of your choice for a monthly fee which will help with the cost of the animal’s health, recovery and rehabilitation. You’ll receive regular update on how ‘your’ animal is progressing, right up to the release stage! As part of this programme, you’ll receive a certificate of adoption and an information pack on your animal.

Donate to help Animal Welfare

The organisations we support are desperately in need of constant funding for day-to-day expenses and funds to ensure that they can grow and continue to do more for animals in need. If you don’t have the time to get directly involved, your donation will be hugely appreciated and go directly to your NGO of choice.
